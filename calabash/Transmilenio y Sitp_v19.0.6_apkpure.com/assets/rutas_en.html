Routes

You must enter -Routes- there you will find the buses, select the one you need and the routes will appear. On top right of the screen you will find three points where you can filter the services that are in "operation" or "all".

Bus schedules:

* L-V: Monday to Friday.

* L-S: Monday to Saturday.

*   S:   Saturday.

* D-F: Sundays and Holidays.

* L-D: Monday to Sunday.
