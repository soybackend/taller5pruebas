Search routes

Do not you know what route to take to get to your destination? Choose the route you are looking for with any of our options, you must choose the transportation system in which you want to move.

1. Set point of origin and point of destination:

Press on the button of point of origin or point of destination will leave a series of options that are:

* Your location: Pressing this option allows you to choose your current location as a starting point or destination point.
* Select site on map: Here you will be able to choose a starting point that we select within the map.

Home points and favorites allowed you to save the location you want to later use them to calculate a route. You can also add other points that you want to select.

2. More options:

It will allow you to find other ways to calculate a route
* Lines: You must select the line in which you are and the station, then perform the same procedure to select the destination.
* Google Maps: You must go to the area of origin and select the station, then perform the same procedure to select the destination station.
* System map: From the system map you must select the origin and destination station.
