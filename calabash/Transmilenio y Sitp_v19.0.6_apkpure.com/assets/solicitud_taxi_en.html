Taxi request

To request taxi service you must press the button that says "Taxis libres", It is necessary to be authenticated in the application, if you are not there you can do it. You will have to activate the GPS of the device, move the pin on the map and complete the address where you want the taxi to arrive. Then you should press the button that says "REQUEST SERVICE" When the driver confirms the service you will receive an email and text message with the driver's information and security code.
