Stations

To find the stations of the system you must enter -Stations- and you will find their respective classification. There you will see the routes that stop at each station or stop, if you are looking for a specific station you can use the search engine. In the upper right part of the screen you will find the search engine , the GPS that when selecting it will show the stations and stops that are close to your location and the map will show you the stations and stops of the system on Google Maps.
